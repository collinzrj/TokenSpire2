## Enemy Knowledge
- **Living Fog**: 80 HP. Summons Gas Bombs (10 HP, 8 damage). Applies "Smoggy" (1 skill/turn). Minions die when leader dies. KILL LEADER ASAP.
- **Gas Bomb**: 10 HP minions, 8 damage. Die when leader dies.
- **Corpse Slug**: RavenousPower(4). When one dies, others eat corpse → stunned + gain 4 Strength. Chain-kill = free stun turns. Whirlwind excellent here to kill multiple simultaneously.
- **Sewer Clam**: 56 HP. Plating (starts 8, -1/turn). Buffs Strength +4 repeatedly. MUST kill fast. Vulnerable critical to break through Plating.
- **Seapunk**: 45 HP. Moderate. Manageable.
- **Toadpole**: Buffs (Thorns +2, Strength). Kill buffer first. Thorns = 2 per attack hit.
- **Sludge Spinner**: 37 HP. Attacks + Weak + Strength buffs. Kill fast.
- **Fossil Stalker**: 51 HP. SuckPower(3) = gains Strength when dealing UNBLOCKED damage. MUST block fully every turn or it snowballs. Patient fight - block first, damage with leftover.
- **Terror Eel (ELITE)**: 140 HP. Stuns at ≤50% HP. Applies Vulnerable 99 after stun. Must block perfectly post-Vuln.
- **Waterfall Giant (BOSS)**: 250 HP. SteamEruptionPower escalates each turn (+3 per turn). Pattern: Buff → Attack+Debuff+Buff → Attack+Buff → Heal+Buff → repeat with escalating damage (10→13→15→20→25→30+). Applies Weak and Vulnerable. **CRITICAL**: Damage escalates to 25-30+ by turn 8-10. MUST have Strength scaling or will be overwhelmed. Without scaling, ~8 damage/turn vs 250 HP = 30+ turns = impossible to survive escalating attacks.

## Card Evaluation (Ironclad)
### S-Tier (Always Take):
- **Shrug It Off**: 8 block + draw. Best common skill. Take 2-3 copies.
- **Pommel Strike**: 9 damage + draw. Strict Strike upgrade.
- **Battle Trance**: 0 cost, draw 3. Incredible hand reload. Play BEFORE Shrug (draw order matters).
- **Whirlwind**: X energy AOE. Amazing vs multi-enemy AND single target when dumping energy.

### A-Tier (Usually Take):
- **Headbutt**: 9 damage + tutor from discard. Great for recurring Bash/key cards.
- **Twin Strike**: 5×2 for 1 energy. Better than Strike, good with Juggling/Vulnerable.
- **Rage**: 0 cost, +3 block per attack. Amazing with attack-heavy hands.
- **Bloodletting**: 0 cost, +1 energy, -3 HP. Big turn enabler.

### B-Tier (Situational):
- **Stampede**: Free attack at end of turn. Decent but unreliable (may pick Whirlwind at 0 energy = wasted).
- **Juggling**: Copy of 3rd attack. Needs attack-dense hands to trigger consistently. Slow setup.
- **Vicious**: Draw on Vulnerable application. Only triggers with Bash - too narrow.
- **Blood Wall**: 16 block for 2 energy + 2 HP. Good when healthy.

### SKIP:
- **Bully, Tremble, Molten Fist, Perfected Strike, Setup Strike**: All bad.

## Strategic Lessons

### #1: STRENGTH SCALING IS NON-NEGOTIABLE
- 4 runs, 0 Strength sources, 4 defeats. Without scaling, boss fights are unwinnable.
- Prioritize Strength cards over defense/utility. Even expensive ones (2 energy powers) are worth it.
- Buy Strength from shops even at premium prices.

### #2: PATHING
- Check ALL downstream paths before choosing rooms.
- Shop > Rest when you have gold AND shop avoids dangerous paths.
- Avoid Elites below 60% HP.
- Pre-boss: need Shop(buy) → Rest(heal) → Boss at near full HP.
- Run 4 pathing was good (avoided Elites, hit shops, rest before boss). Keep this approach.

### #3: EVENT REROLL TRAP
- Lost 25 HP (3+4+5+6+7) rerolling event to remove a Strike. Maximum 2-3 rerolls before HP cost exceeds benefit. Set HARD LIMIT: stop rerolling after 12 HP spent total.

### #4: HP MANAGEMENT
- Block fully vs scaling enemies (Fossil Stalker SuckPower, Sewer Clam Strength).
- Use Whirlwind to end multi-enemy fights fast (Corpse Slug simultaneous kill).
- Burning Blood (+6) helps but isn't enough to cover sloppy play.

### #5: POWER SETUP TIMING
- Boss free turns (Heal+Buff) are rare and precious. Play ALL powers on these turns.
- Don't play 3 powers over 3 turns - play damage/block powers together when safe.
- Run 4: Had Vicious, Juggling, Stampede but they took 4+ turns to set up. By then, boss was dealing 25+.

### #6: BOSS PREPARATION
- Need 15+ block/turn sustained AND 15+ damage/turn sustained for Waterfall Giant.
- With 3 energy: ideal is 1 block card (8-16) + 1 Strength-boosted attack (15+) per turn.
- Deck needs to average blocking incoming while still killing 250 HP in ~12 turns.

### #7: USE POTIONS PROACTIVELY
- Don't save all potions for boss. Use in hallway fights to preserve HP.
- Simple potions (Weak, Block) > Complex potions (Power, Colorless) due to activation issues.

## Combat Priorities
1. Check incoming damage FIRST
2. At ≤20 HP: block is ALWAYS priority - NO exceptions
3. At 20-40 HP: block fully, damage with leftover
4. At 40+ HP: can be aggressive if it shortens fight
5. Enemy buffing (no attack): go all-in on damage/setup
6. Play draw cards (Shrug, Battle Trance) BEFORE committing energy to maximize options
7. Battle Trance BEFORE Shrug (BT prevents draws; use Shrug draw first if both in hand)
8. Headbutt: always put Bash or Battle Trance on top unless specific need
